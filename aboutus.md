# Phan tich thiet ke trang ladipage.vn (tham khao)

## Tong quan thong diep va bo cuc
- Hero mo phong "all-in-one" cho marketing & sales, tap trung loi ich tang truong va toi uu van hanh.
- Nhieu tang thong tin tu tong quan -> san pham -> giai phap theo nganh -> bang chung xa hoi -> case study -> footer thong tin cong ty.
- CTA xuat hien nhieu lan o dau trang va trong moi khoi san pham (dang ky, dung thu, xem chi tiet).

## Cau truc section cap cao (thu tu giong trang)
1. Header + nav: menu san pham/giai phap/tai nguyen + CTA dang nhap/dang ky.
2. Hero claim: thong diep lon ve tang truong + CTA chinh.
3. Social proof: so lieu su dung lon (k+), logo/thuong hieu tin dung.
4. San pham chinh: khoi "Nen tang toan dien" voi danh sach danh muc.
5. Feature cards: moi san pham con co 3-5 loi ich ngan gon + CTA.
6. Giai phap theo nganh: cac khoi theo tung linh vuc + bullet loi ich.
7. Chi so van hanh: cac con so ve so landing page, luu tru, automation.
8. Tin nhiem & reliability: bao mat, hieu suat, de dung, ket noi da kenh.
9. Case study/Testimonials: nguoi that, vai tro, bai toan, giai phap.
10. CTA chot: bat dau mien phi, dat lich tu van.
11. Footer: sitemap da cot + thong tin cong ty + policy.

## He thong thi giac
- He mau chinh xanh duong + nen sang; CTA dung mau tuong phan, thuong la xanh dam.
- Text headings dam, size lon; body text trung tinh de de doc.
- Khoi noi dung co boder/box shadow nhe de phan tach.
- Layout nhieu grid 2-3 cot; card co icon/illustration.

## Navigation va CTA
- Nav bar dinh (sticky) voi menu dropdown theo nhom (san pham/giai phap/tai nguyen).
- CTA chinh: Dang ky/Dung thu mien phi; CTA phu: Xem chi tiet, Kham pha.
- CTA lap lai sau moi khoi de giam friction.

## Component chi tiet (goi y cho agent)
### 1) Header/Nav
- Logo trai, menu giua, CTA phai.
- Dropdown co cot: danh muc + link nhanh + chu thich ngan.
- Nut CTA dang ky; link dang nhap phu.

### 2) Hero
- Headline cap 1 nhan manh loi ich tang truong.
- Subtext 1-2 dong ve giai phap tong the.
- 1 CTA chinh + 1 CTA phu.
- Nen co illustration hoac hinh san pham.

### 3) Social proof
- Dong so lieu lon: 700k+ khach hang, so landing page, so kich ban.
- Doi tac/thuong hieu (logo strip).

### 4) Feature grid "Nen tang toan dien"
- Tabs/segmented control cho danh muc (Marketing, CRM, Automation...).
- Moi tab co card list: Ten san pham, 3-4 bullet loi ich, CTA.

### 5) Solution by industry
- Card theo nganh: Tuyen sinh, Dao tao, Ban le, F&B, Su kien.
- Moi card co 3-4 bullet benefit.

### 6) Reliability strip
- 3-4 card ngan: Bao mat cao, De dung, Ket noi lien mach, Tu dong hoa.

### 7) Case study slider
- Card testimonial: Anh/ten/vai tro, bai toan, giai phap, link doc them.

### 8) Footer
- 4-5 cot: San pham, Giai phap, Tai nguyen, Cong ty, Social.
- Thong tin cong ty + policy.

## Guidance ve microcopy
- H1 dong nhat loi ich chinh (tang truong, toi uu van hanh).
- H2 dan vao danh muc san pham, tap trung vao ket qua.
- CTA dung dong tu hanh dong: Dang ky, Dung thu, Dat lich.

## Tone of voice (goi y)
- Tin cay, chuyen nghiep, thuc dung; uu tien loi ich cua phu huynh va hoc vien.
- Ngan gon, ro rang, nhan manh ket qua (tien bo, minh bach, dong hanh).
- Trai nghiem than thien, khong qua mang quang cao; tranh phong cach khoe khoang.
- Dung dong tu hanh dong manh: Dat, Dang ky, Nhan tu van, Xem lo trinh.
- Viet theo cach huong dan, co dong luc, tap trung su an tam va chat luong.

## Wireframe goi y (About Us page)
1. Hero
	- H1 + subtext 2 dong.
	- 2 CTA: Dat tu van / Xem quy trinh.
	- Hinh anh giao vien/hoc vien.
2. Su menh - Tam nhin
	- 2 cot: mo ta + so lieu tin cay.
3. Quy trinh chon loc gia su
	- Timeline 3-5 buoc (so buoc + mo ta ngan).
4. Cam ket chat luong
	- 3-4 card: minh bach hoc phi, bao cao dinh ky, doi gia su.
5. He sinh thai ho tro
	- Bullet ve cong cu, nhan vien dieu phoi, tai nguyen hoc tap.
6. Doi tac va cong dong
	- Logo strip + trich dan nho tu phu huynh/hoc vien.
7. CTA ket thuc
	- H2 + nut Dat tu van, link FAQ.
8. Footer
	- Link nhanh + thong tin lien he.

## SEO Content (goi y cho trang About Us cua nen tang dat gia su)
### Title
- About Us | Nen tang dat gia su uy tin va minh bach

### Meta description
- Tim hieu ve chung toi: doi ngu giao duc tan tam, quy trinh chon loc gia su nghiem ngat, va he sinh thai ho tro phu huynh hoc vien toi uu.

### H1
- Ve chung toi: Ket noi gia su chat luong va hoc vien phu hop

### H2 goi y
- Su menh va tam nhin
- Quy trinh chon loc gia su
- Cam ket chat luong va an toan
- He sinh thai ho tro phu huynh va hoc vien
- Doi tac va cong dong giao duc

### Doan gioi thieu (SEO-friendly)
- Chung toi la nen tang ket noi gia su va hoc vien, tap trung vao chat luong giang day, su minh bach ve hoc phi, va hieu qua tien bo. Doi ngu tuyen chon gia su qua nhieu vong danh gia, dong thoi theo doi tien do hoc tap dinh ky de dam bao ket qua on dinh.

### Bullet cam ket
- Chon loc gia su co chuyen mon va ky nang su pham.
- Minh bach hoc phi va lo trinh hoc tap.
- Ho tro doi gia su khi can, khong lam gian doan tien trinh.
- Bao cao dinh ky cho phu huynh va hoc vien.

### FAQ snippet (phuc vu SEO)
- Gia su co the day thu khong? Co, ban co the dat 1-2 buoi hoc thu truoc khi xac nhan.
- Mat bao lau de ghep lop? Thuong 24-48 gio tuy mon hoc va khu vuc.
- Co doi gia su khi khong phu hop? Co, trung tam ho tro dieu phoi nhanh.

### Keywords goi y
- dat gia su, gia su chat luong, tim gia su uy tin, hoc phi gia su, nen tang gia su
